Mission and Vision of the Department

The Economics Department at Harley Davidson University is dedicated to providing an innovative, rigorous, and inclusive academic experience that equips students with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in a rapidly changing global economy. The department’s mission is to foster a deep understanding of economic principles, theories, and practices while encouraging students to apply their learning to real-world challenges. By combining cutting-edge research, critical thinking, and practical applications, the department prepares students to become leaders in both the public and private sectors, empowering them to contribute meaningfully to the advancement of society. A central goal of the department is to cultivate analytical skills that enable students to assess complex economic situations and make informed decisions. Through a curriculum that integrates both macroeconomic and microeconomic perspectives, students gain a holistic view of economic systems and their impact on individuals, businesses, and governments. Harley Davidson University’s Economics Department strives to create a dynamic learning environment that nurtures intellectual curiosity, promotes ethical decision-making, and encourages collaboration among students, faculty, and the broader community. Faculty members are committed to providing high-quality instruction and mentoring, creating an engaging classroom atmosphere where students can actively participate in discussions, share diverse perspectives, and develop their problem-solving abilities. By fostering a culture of inquiry and exploration, the department ensures that students are not only well-prepared for careers in economics but also for leadership roles in policy-making, business, finance, and international relations. The department also emphasizes the importance of interdisciplinary learning, recognizing that the study of economics intersects with various fields such as political science, sociology, history, and environmental studies.
In line with its mission, the Economics Department’s vision is to be a recognized leader in the development of thought-provoking, globally relevant economic education. The department envisions producing graduates who possess a profound understanding of economic dynamics and who are equipped to tackle the pressing challenges facing the world today, such as economic inequality, sustainability, and the evolving nature of work in a digital age. Through its commitment to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement, the department aims to shape future economists who are not only adept at navigating the complexities of the global market but also capable of driving positive change. The vision includes fostering an inclusive academic environment where diverse perspectives are valued and where students from all backgrounds are given the opportunity to succeed. In addition to academic excellence, the department envisions a strong focus on cutting-edge research that addresses contemporary economic issues. Faculty members are actively involved in both theoretical and applied research that informs economic policy, business practices, and societal well-being. By encouraging students to engage in research projects and internships, the department ensures that its graduates are not only well-versed in academic theory but also prepared to apply their knowledge in practical settings. This hands-on approach to learning enhances the employability of graduates and equips them with the skills necessary to adapt to an ever-evolving job market.
Furthermore, the Economics Department at Harley Davidson University is committed to global engagement, recognizing the interconnectedness of economies worldwide. The department strives to provide students with opportunities to study abroad, participate in international conferences, and collaborate with institutions across the globe. By embracing a global perspective, the department ensures that students are well-prepared to work in diverse economic environments and contribute to international policy discussions. Ultimately, the mission and vision of the Economics Department at Harley Davidson University are centered on fostering a learning environment that prioritizes academic excellence, practical experience, and global engagement. By providing students with the tools to understand and navigate the complexities of the global economy, the department aims to produce graduates who are not only skilled economists but also responsible and innovative leaders capable of addressing the challenges of the 21st century.
