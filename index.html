Meet the Department of Economics

The Department of Economics at the University of Harley Davidson (ECHDU), within the Faculty of Social Sciences (FoSS), is committed to delivering high-quality education and advancing research in various economic disciplines. ECHDU offers a range of degree programs, including a 4-year Bachelor of Economics, a 1.5-year Master of Economics, a 2-year M.Phil, and Ph.D. programs in Economics. Additionally, we offer a 1.5-year Professional Master's in Economic Policy and Data Analytics (PMEPDA).
